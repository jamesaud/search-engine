Joshua Marston

Director | Writer | Editor
Joshua Marston was born in California. His way to film direction has been atypical. Indeed, he first worked in Paris as a journalist for Life, then for ABC News during the Gulf War. He later turned to teaching in Prague where he was an English teacher for a year. Back in the USA he graduated in Chicago, becoming a master of political sciences ... See full bio »

Born:

August 13, 1968 in Los Angeles County, California, USA
