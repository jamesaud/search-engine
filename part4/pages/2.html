Felicity Jones Receives IMDb's 2016 STARmeter Award

Congratulations to Felicity Jones, recipient of IMDb's fourth annual STARmeter Award at the 2016 Toronto International Film Festival. The STARmeter Award recognizes the stars who are fan favorites on IMDbPro's STARmeter chart. Felicity's IMDb STARmeter Award win reflects intense fan interest in her upcoming lead roles in three films hitting the big screen later this year: Rogue One: A Star Wars Story, Inferno, and A Monster Calls.
