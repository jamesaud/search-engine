Cannes 2016

Cannes International Film Festival

  | 

May 11-22, 2016
I, Daniel Blake
American Honey

The 69th annual Cannes Film Festival was held from May 11 - May 22, 2016. Ken Loach's I, Daniel Blake received the Palme d'Or, while Andrea Arnold's American Honey took home the Jury Prize award as well as the Prize of the Ecumenical Jury - Special Mention.
